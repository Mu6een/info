Know about School of Computer science

    School of Computer Science presently offers the courses namely Master of Computer Applications (MCA)
and Doctor of Philosophy in Computer Science (Ph.D). The M.Sc. in Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence)
will be started from academic year (2021-2022).
    The learner centric approach is ensured through student seminars, assignments, project work, visits etc.
making students a part of the research done by the faculty and publications in refereed journals.
Syllabus has been designed keeping in view requirement of the industry and regularly updated with
the latest technology. Innovative Student Projects with new ideas is part of curriculum.
